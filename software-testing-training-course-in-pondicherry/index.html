    Manual Testing

    Manually testing software goods and applications is considered to be one of the most basic methods of evaluating software platforms. Manual testing is the process of testing software/software products manually, i.e. by a Qa Analyst or a Testing Engineer, rather than using automated testing tools. The test plan is mostly carried out to look for any flaws, bugs, problems, or faults in the software product or the functioning of the software/software application throughout the development phase and the testing phase.

    Automation testing

    The primary goal of conducting Automation Testing is to reduce time while also ensuring the credibility of the product being tested. Selenium Grid makes it simple for testers to run many tests in a lateral fashion, thus reducing the amount of time spent on test execution. Aside from that, it is a cost-effective solution to adopt since it does not need any licence fees. Furthermore, it is straightforward and simple to understand and utilise. Selenium also offers a large network of testers from which you may benefit from ongoing upgrades, changes, and assistance, as well as other resources.
