You Will Never Think That Learning AWS Could Be So Beneficial! But It Is!



Before 10 years, we have to physically go to the store or the company to buy or sell things. But now, we can buy or sell anything from the comfort of our homes and did not need to move an inch. That is the power of online business and cloud computing technology.

In the last few years, all the types of business are moving from traditional direct approach to the advanced online approach. Combined with the benefits of cloud computing such as flexibility, scalability and security, online business model is becoming popular and successful. Many tech giants like IBM, Microsoft and Google are offering cloud computing services to help the companies shift to online business models.

Amazon Web Services (AWS) is one of the few popular and commonly used cloud computing platforms for online businesses. Therefore, learning AWS will not only help in getting a job but also be more beneficial than you ever thought. AWS has numerous benefits and because of these benefits, AWS professionals will get unimaginable advantages compared to other software professionals. All the benefits of AWS training in Bangalore are listed below:

Huge demand

Cloud computing is growing at an exponential rate and creating a huge demand for cloud professionals like AWS Developers and Administrators. According to Forbes, Cloud computing has provided around 20 million jobs worldwide in 2017. This is only going to increase since the spending on cloud related services and infrastructure is increasing every year worldwide. So, going to AWS training in Bangalore will increase your job opportunities immediately and exponentially.

Highest Pay

According to Forbes list of Top paying certifications in 2017, AWS Certified Solutions Architect is the third highest paying certification. AWS professionals with that certification will get a salary of 125,000 USD per annum. Even freshers with nil experience will get around 20,000 USD per annum. Yes, you read it right, in Indian Rupees, Professionals with certification and freshers will get 80lakhs and 12lakhs per annum if they learn AWS and get a job in AWS domain. Therefore, getting an AWS Certification in Bangalore will certainly increase your salary many folds.

Universal use

AWS is used in almost all the sectors ranging from manufacturing to services sector. Because of its universal use, AWS professionals have countless job opportunities in almost all the sectors. Some of the companies using AWS are BMW, Lamborghini, Tata Motors, British Gas, Unilever, Novartis, Pfizer, Canon, Samsung, Sony, Hitachi, Xiaomi, Alcatel-Lucent, Vodafone Italy, Autodesk, Adobe Systems, Ubisoft, Citrix, Siemens, Harvard Medical School, Coinbase, Coursera, Airbnb, Quantas, Dow Jones, Financial Times, Channel 4, Guardian News & Media, Hearst Corporation, The Weather Company, IMDb, JustGiving, Lonely Planet, Lyft, News International, Nordstrom, Pinterest, Spotify, WeTransfer and Zynga. The diversity of the sectors in which these companies operate will tell you the importance of joining AWS course in Bangalore to increase the number of possible domains for your career.

Prestigious jobs

AWS is used by prestigious and important organizations and companies around the world. Some of these organizations are NASA, US Department of State, UK Ministry of Justice, European Space Agency and International Civil Aviation Organization. And the esteemed companies using AWS are General Electric, Capital One Financials, Johnson & Johnson, McDonald’s, Kellogg’s and Netflix. Therefore, enrolling in AWS training in Bangalore will help you get a job in these enterprises which are an epic opportunity for any software professional.

Market Leader

AWS along with Microsoft Azure is the market leader in providing cloud services which means learning AWS will help you stay ahead of all the cloud professionals. According to Gartner’s famous Magic Quadrant report, AWS is staying ahead of all other cloud service providers in both the aspects of innovation and execution related to the cloud computing domain.

Highest remuneration for Freelancing

AWS professionals not only have highest paying jobs but also have the highest rate in the freelancing jobs worldwide. According to a survey by Code Mentor, an online outsourcing platform, AWS professionals average the highest hourly rate, around 74USD per hour which is 4500INR per hour, among all other freelance professionals. So, if you want to earn a huge income just from the comfort of home, AWS course in Bangalore will be your best choice.

Variety of skills

AWS offers around 70 services and thus there are many different skills required in AWS domain. For example, persons with good networking experience can go for Advanced Networking – Specialty certification to enter the AWS networking domain and persons who have passion for data analytics can do Big Data – Specialty certification to get a job in Big Data domain. Like this, there are five different certifications with each one focusing on a specific skillset. So, you can choose the certification based on your skill and experience and then go on to work on the domain that you like. Only the AWS training will give you such freedom inside a single platform (i.e.) AWS.

You would have never imagined that leaning AWS will have the benefits listed above. But, learning AWS at the correct place is also matter a lot because the recruiters see the reputation of the place you learned before selecting you for interviews. So, choose the best training institute in Bangalore for learning AWS like the Upshot Technologies in BTM, Bangalore.
