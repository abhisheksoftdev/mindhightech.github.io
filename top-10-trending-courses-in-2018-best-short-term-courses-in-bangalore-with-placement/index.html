Top 10 trending courses in 2021? Best short term courses in Bangalore with placement?



Different domains in the software industry are going through major improvement and advancements in the last few years. And these improvements and advancements can lead to numerous employment opportunities for persons with suitable skills. Now, the following 10 courses can teach the suitable skills and empower the professionals to make use of the employment opportunities. And for each course, the best short term course in Bangalore with placement is given below.

Machine Learning

The field of machine learning is growing exponentially but it is seriously understaffed due to the lack of specialist professionals. Finding the suitable software professionals with both skills and experience in machine learning is very difficult and that is why there is a great demand for Machine learning course throughout the world.

The best short term machine learning course with placement in Bangalore is “Machine Learning with R Training" by Upshot technologies. It is a six-week course which covers the basics of machine learning and the use of R language in it.

Data Science

With the rise of big data, data scientists are becoming a key component in many domains because they can get valuable insights from inconsequential data. Some of the popular domains include finance and banking, healthcare and sales and marketing. Companies are investing on a big scale to recruit talented Data scientists from all over the world.

Upshot technologies in Bangalore is providing four different short term courses on data science which are the best courses with placement in Bangalore. All are six-week courses which will definitely get you placed as soon as possible.

Mobile Application Development

Mobile application developers are already in high demand because of the rapid increase in the usage of smartphones in the recent years. The demand will only get greater in 2018 as the number of smartphone users is only increasing in 2018. Many companies are recruiting app developers with fluency in multiple languages namely Java, HTML, Objective-C, C++, Python, and Swift.

Mobile application training courses given by Upshot technologies in Bangalore are the best short term courses with placement on Mobile app development. All the courses are for six weeks and will always ensure that all the students are placed.

Robotic Process Automation

Robotic Process Automation (RPA) is one of the emerging domains in the software industry because of the recent developments in the artificial intelligence and machine learning domains. It can automate applications based on the rules given in the software. There are more than a dozen vendors in this domain and Blue prism is one of the leading vendors among them.

The Blue Prism training course given by Upshot technologies in Bangalore is the best short term on Robotic Process Automation in and around Bangalore. It is a six-week course which infuses all the skills and knowledge required to have a great career in RPA.

DevOps

DevOps is one of the latest software development frameworks but the most effective one yet because of its concept of collaboration and the reduction of time required. It strongly advocates automation of all the possible steps in the software development lifecycle to speed up the process. Because of its success, many companies are investing a lot in DevOps.

There are six DevOps-related training courses provided by Upshot technologies with each one concentrating on the specific tool used in DevOps. All the six courses are of the same duration (six-weeks) and have the ability to get the students placed.

Blockchain

Blockchain is one of the latest internet-related technologies developed in the last 10 years. Though it was developed in 2009, it received huge attention after the prices of Bitcoin soar to great heights in 2015. Now, blockchain technology is used in many other domains which use public distributed networks to maintain records.

The best short term course in Bangalore with placement for Blockchain technology is the Block Chain Training course by Upshot technology. It is a six-week course which provides free placement training after the course to help the students get placed.

AWS Redshift

AWS Redshift is a fast and huge data warehouse service that was built on massive parallel processing (MPP) technology. It is simple to use and cheaper than all other competitors and it has faster data analysing tools. It is also compatible with external data analysis tools like IBM Cognos and Informatica. That is why many companies are switching to Redshift lately.

AWS Redshift training by Upshot technologies in Bangalore is the best short term course with placement for AWS Redshift. This six-week course also has the highest success ratio (i.e.) 100% placement record in all its batches.

Hadoop

Since the arrival of big data, the search for the efficient data analytics tool for big data is going on. Hadoop is the most effective tool yet for the handling of big data which not only includes analytics but also formatting, storing, and retrieving the data. Many big corporations, research institutions, government agencies, and NGOs are adopting Hadoop in the last few years.

The best short term course with placement for Hadoop in Bangalore is provided by Upshot technologies in BTM Layout. The course is for six weeks only but most of the students had mastered Hadoop before getting placed.

Windows Azure

Windows Azure is a cloud computing service provided by Microsoft Corporation and it is the second most used after AWS. It provides all three services – infrastructure (IaaS), platform (PaaS) and service (SaaS) through the cloud. It is available in 36 regions all over the world and the only cloud service provider with facilities in the African continent.

Windows Azure training by Upshot technologies is the best short term course with placement in Bangalore for Windows Azure. It is a six-week course and it has the highest possible placement record of all time (100% placement) in Bangalore.

SAS

Advanced analytics is one of the important sub-domains in the data analytics domain because it can employ complex methods for better results and reduce the human intervention. SAS is the most used tools in advanced analytics and it has 35% of the total clients in advanced analytics domain. It is also the largest independent vendor in the business intelligence software.

Upshot technologies in BTM is providing three training courses on SAS which are the best short term courses with placement for SAS, in Bangalore. All three are for six weeks and have a unique record of 100% placement record in all its batches, until now.
